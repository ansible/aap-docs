= Validate the target environment

[role="_abstract"]
Verify that all {PlatformNameShort} services are running, credentials work correctly, and migrated content like projects, inventories, and job templates are accessible on {OCPShort}.

.Procedure
. Verify all migrated components are functional.
. Test workflows and automation processes.
. Validate user access and permissions.
. Confirm content synchronization and availability.
. Test integration with {OCPShort}-specific features.
