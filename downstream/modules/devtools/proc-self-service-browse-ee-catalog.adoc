= Browse the {ExecEnvShort} catalog

[role="_abstract"]
Browse the {ExecEnvShort} catalog to find and manage EE definitions in {SelfServiceShort}.
The catalog displays {ExecEnvShort} definitions discovered from your synchronized repositories, allowing you to view their configurations and access their files.
Use search and filtering to locate specific {ExecEnvShort}s.

.Prerequisites

* You have logged in to {SelfServiceShort}.
* Repositories containing {ExecEnvShort} definitions have been synced to the portal.

.Procedure

. Navigate to the {ExecEnvShort} catalog.
+
[NOTE]
====
Navigation path and catalog interface details will be documented here with screenshots.
====
+
. Browse or search for {ExecEnvShort} definitions.
+
[NOTE]
====
Search, filter, and sort options will be documented here with screenshots.
====
+
. View detailed information about an {ExecEnvShort} definition.
+
[NOTE]
====
Detail view contents and available options will be documented here with screenshots.
====
+
. To download an EE definition, click *Download EE files*.
+
The EE definition files download as a `.tar` archive.

.Verification

. Verify you can locate your expected {ExecEnvShort} definitions in the catalog.
. If expected EEs are missing, verify the source repository has been synced successfully and contains valid Backstage catalog entity files.
