= Enabling feedback to Red Hat

[role="_abstract"]
Enable the optional feedback form to allow users to submit suggestions and general feedback directly through the portal interface.

.Prerequisites

* You have administrative access to the {OCPShort} console.
* The {SelfService} is installed in an OpenShift project.

.Procedure

. Log in to the {OCPShort} console.
. In the *Developer* perspective, navigate to *Helm*.
. Click the *More actions* icon for your self-service automation portal Helm release and select *Upgrade*.
. Select *YAML view*.
. Locate the `ansible` section and set the `feedback.enabled` value to `true`:
+
----
ansible:
  feedback:
    enabled: true
----
. Click btn:[Upgrade]

.Verification

. Log in to the {SelfService}.
. Confirm that the Feedback button is visible in the bottom-left corner of the console.
