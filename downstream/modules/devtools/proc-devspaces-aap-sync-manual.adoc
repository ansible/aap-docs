= Manually triggering synchronization

[role="_abstract"]
{PlatformNameShort} administrators can manually trigger synchronization from the portal UI.

.Procedure

. Log in to {SelfService} as an {PlatformNameShort} Administrator.
. Navigate to the *Synchronization management* page.
. Click btn:[Manual sync] for Organizations or Job Templates.
. Monitor the sync progress in the UI.

.Verification

Verify that the synchronization is listed as *Successful* on the *Synchronization management* page.
