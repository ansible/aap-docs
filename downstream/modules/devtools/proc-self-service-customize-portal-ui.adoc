= Enabling custom support URL

[role="_abstract"]
Update the Helm configuration to redirect the default support link to your organization's specific support resources.

.Prerequisites

* You have administrative access to the {OCPShort} console.
* The {SelfService} is installed in an OpenShift project.

.Procedure

. Log in to the {OCPShort} console.
. In the *Developer* perspective, navigate to *Helm*.
. Click the *More actions* icon for your self-service automation portal Helm release and select *Upgrade*.
. Select *YAML view*.
. Add the `CUSTOMER_SUPPORT_URL` environment variable to the `extraEnvVars` section:
+
----
redhat-developer-hub:
  upstream:
    backstage:
      extraEnvVars:
        - name: CUSTOMER_SUPPORT_URL
          value: https://your-support-portal.example.com
----
. Click btn:[Upgrade].

.Verification

. Log in to the {SelfService}.
. Hover over the *Support* link in the upper right of the UI (next to the *Create* icon) and verify it points to your custom URL.
