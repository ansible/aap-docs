= Exporting automation reports

[role="_abstract"]
You can export your automation usage and ROI data as CSV or PDF files to share with stakeholders.

.Prerequisites

* You must have configured your **Monthly AAP cost** and **Hourly rate** in the settings to ensure accurate ROI data in the exported report.

.Procedure

. Select a saved report or use the filters to customize the current view.
. Select a **Time period** for the data you want to export.
. Click **Export** and select one of the following formats:
* **Export as CSV**: Generates a spreadsheet of the raw data.
* **Save as PDF**: Generates a formatted summary report. 
. If you select **Save as PDF**, you must specify a start and end date in the **Date Range** selector.
. Click **Save**.

.Verification

* Open the downloaded file to verify that the metrics and date ranges match your dashboard configuration.
