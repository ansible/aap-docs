= Add execution environment containers to your container registry


[role="_abstract"]
Pull an execution environment from an external registry to your own registry. 

When your execution environment is in your registry, you can then provision access and customize it for your users.

.Procedure

. Pull an {ExecEnvShort} from an external registry (like registry.redhat.io) to your local machine.

. Tag the image locally for your {PrivateHubName} registry.

. Push the image to your {PrivateHubName}.

. Configure access permissions and documentation (such as READMEs) within {PrivateHubName} so your teams can use the image.
