= Verifying installation of {PlatformNameShort}

[role="_abstract"]
Perform the verification step for your {PlatformName} deployment by successfully logging into the platform. A successful login confirms that the installation is complete, ensuring your environment is ready for operational use.

.Procedure

* Log in to {PlatformName}. 
+
[IMPORTANT]
====
If the installation fails and you are a customer who has purchased a valid license for {PlatformName}, contact Ansible through the link:https://docs.redhat.com/[Red Hat Customer portal].
====
