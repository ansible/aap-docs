= Configure your {HubName} operator route options

[role="_abstract"]

The {PlatformName} operator installation form allows you to further configure your {HubName} operator route options under *Advanced configuration*.

.Procedure

. Log in to {OCP}.
. Navigate to menu:Operators[Installed Operators].
. Select your {OperatorPlatformNameShort} deployment.
. Select the *Ansible Automation Platform* tab.
. Click the {MoreActionsIcon} icon next to your {PlatformNameShort} instance and select btn:[Edit AnsibleAutomationPlatform].
. Click btn:[YAML view] and locate the `spec.hub:` section.
. Configure the route options under the `hub:` section:
+
[source,yaml]
----
spec:
  hub:
    ingress_type: Route
    route_host: hub.example.com  # Custom hostname for the route
    route_tls_termination_mechanism: Edge  # Options: Edge, Passthrough
    route_tls_secret: hub-tls-secret  # Optional: TLS credential secret
----
+
. Click btn:[Save].
+
[NOTE]
====
Edge termination is recommended for most instances. After configuring your route, you can customize additional route settings by adding them to the `hub:` section in the {PlatformNameShort} custom resource.

For more examples of {PlatformNameShort} custom resources, see link:{BaseURL}/red_hat_ansible_automation_platform/{PlatformVers}/html-single/installing_on_openshift_container_platform/index#appendix-operator-crs_operator-platform-doc[Appendix: {PlatformName} custom resources].
====
