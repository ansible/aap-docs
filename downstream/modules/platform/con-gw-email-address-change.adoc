= Email address change restrictions

The platform restricts email address modifications because external authenticators can use email addresses to identify users.

Non-admin users cannot change their {PlatformNameShort} email address. 
Only administrators manage email address changes to prevent unauthorized account manipulation.

The following users can change email addresses:

* Platform administrators (superusers)
* Organization administrators, on deployments where Organization admins can manage users and teams is enabled

If a user's email address requires updating, contact a platform administrator to make the change.
