= Create a Team

Use the following procedure to create a new Team:

.Procedure
. On the *Teams* page, click btn:[Add].
+
image:teams-create-new-team.png[Teams -create new team]
. Enter the appropriate details into the following fields:

* *Name*
* Optional: *Description*
* *Organization*: Choose from an existing organization
. Click *Save*.
. The *Details* dialog opens, enabling you to review and edit your Team information.
+
image:teams-example-team-successfully-created.png[Teams- Details dialog]
