= System requirements

[role="_abstract"]
Use this information when planning your {PlatformName} installations and designing {AutomationMesh} topologies that fit your use case.

== Prerequisites

* Obtain root access either through the `sudo` command, or through privilege escalation. 
** De-escalate privileges from root to users such as: AWX, PostgreSQL, {EDAName}, or Pulp.
* Configured an NTP client on all nodes.
