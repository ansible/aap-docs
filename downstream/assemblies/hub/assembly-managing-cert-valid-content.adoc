= Red Hat Certified, validated, and Ansible Galaxy content in {HubName}

:context: cloud-sync

[role="_abstract"]
{CertifiedName} are included in your subscription to {PlatformName}. Using {HubNameMain}, you can access and curate a unique set of collections from all forms of Ansible content.

Red Hat Ansible content contains two types of content that you can use to build your library:

* {CertifiedName}
* {Valid} collections

You can update these collections manually by downloading their packages, or sync content through {Console} .

After you have configured a remote repository, use {HubNameMain} to distribute the relevant {CertifiedColl}s to your users by creating a requirements file.
