= Scheduling Business Continuity tests

[role="_abstract"]
A customer can validate regional failover operations by scheduling a simulation once annually.

[IMPORTANT]
====
To ensure platform stability and maintain operational availability, customers are restricted to scheduling a maximum of one Business Continuity test per calendar year.
====
