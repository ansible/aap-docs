= Existing customers

[role="_abstract"]
Existing customers must migrate their current infrastructure layout to the mandatory MRBC topology. The deadline to complete this migration depends strictly on the customer's contract type:

* *Annual contracts*: Existing customers on an annual contract are required to migrate to the MRBC infrastructure topology by their current contract end date.
* *Monthly contracts*: Existing customers on a monthly subscription plan are granted a 3-month grace period from the official feature launch date to complete the migration.

[IMPORTANT]
====
If an environment has not been migrated by the conclusion of the 3-month grace period or the respective annual contract end date, Red Hat will automatically migrate the infrastructure to the mandatory MRBC topology.
====

.Procedure
. Log in to the Red Hat link:https://access.redhat.com/[Customer Portal].
. Open a support ticket requesting a regional failover to the  secondary deployment footprint.
. Ensure you have selected *{PlatformName} On Clouds* as the product. 
. Use the following formatting exactly for the support ticket subject and body to ensure rapid routing and execution by the Red Hat SRE team.
+
----
SUBJECT: Request for Migration to MRBC Infrastructure

Severity: 3 
BODY:

Hello SRE Team,

I am requesting the migration of my existing managed Ansible Automation Platform environment to the MRBC infrastructure topology.
