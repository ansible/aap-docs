= Initiating a Business Continuity test

[role="_abstract"]
Use the following procedure to initiate a Business Continuity test for your {PlatformName} service deployment.

.Procedure
. Open a standard support ticket through the Red Hat Customer Portal to coordinate the exercise with Red Hat SREs.
. Coordinate with Red Hat SREs to simulate a primary region failure domain event. Red Hat moves the environment to the secondary region only after receiving a customer's explicit confirmation through the support ticket.
** During this simulation window, the application and automation in the environment will experience expected outages.
. Work directly with Red Hat Product Management and Engineering during a scheduled maintenance window following a successful test to complete delta reconciliation and safely restore the primary environment footprint.
