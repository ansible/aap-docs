= New customers

[role="_abstract"]
New customers are required to activate Multi-Region Business Continuity during the onboarding process.

* *Default enablement*: For all new deployments created on or after the feature launch date, MRBC is enabled by default.
* *Required infrastructure*: New environments automatically deploy with the active-passive architecture required to support regional failover.
