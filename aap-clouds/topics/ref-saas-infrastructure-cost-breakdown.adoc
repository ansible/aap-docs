= Infrastructure cost breakdown

[role="_abstract"]
Maintaining the warm standby requires a constant baseline metering in addition to the active region metering.

[cols="3,3,2", options="header"]
|====
| Timeframe | Calculation | Total hours metered
| *Daily* | 2 nodes x 24 hrs | 48 hours
| *Monthly* | 48 hours x 30 days | ~1,440 hours
| *Annually* | 1440hrs x 12 months | ~17,280 hours
|====
